Guinness Record Achievement

  • by:Bharathi Ravi
  • date:February, 2016

During my 10th standard, I had the honor of participating in a Guinness Record project titled "Guinness Record Project of Colored Water Mosaic." This project involved:

Participation

Our school gathered 100 students to participate in this monumental endeavor.

Project Description

Using 200 ml paper cups filled with colored water, we meticulously crafted a mosaic of Gurudev's photo on the floor.

Scale

The mosaic covered an impressive area of over 6300 square feet.

Effort

We dedicated a total of 16 hours, 51 minutes, and 21 seconds to complete this ambitious project.

Result

Ultimately, our efforts culminated in the placement of 100,800 cups to bring this vibrant mosaic to life.

This experience not only fostered teamwork and dedication but also instilled in me the importance of striving for excellence in every endeavor.
