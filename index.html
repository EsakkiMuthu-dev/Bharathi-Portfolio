A Little Girl's First Steps into the Design World!

I'm Bharathi, daughter of Bakyalakshmi Ravi and Ravi Bakyalakshmi, born in the small town of Nagore. From a young age, drawing was my happy place - a way to let my big dreams and wild imagination run free. As the first girl in my family filled with lots of ambitions and responsibilities, I grew up with my younger sister, Poorna Ravi. For me, my family is everything, and our humble beginnings gave us the fire to chase our passions.

After finishing school and college, I started my career as a tester at an MNC company. But design kept calling out to me. With every pencil stroke, I found joy in bringing ideas to life, blending function with looks to create things that felt good to use and easy on the eyes. Drawing has always been my trusty tool to build worlds. Now, I want to use it to craft digital experiences that make people's lives a little better, a little easier, one pixel at a time..
