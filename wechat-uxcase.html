WeChat UI/UX Design Project:
Empowering Academic Learning

  • by:Bharathi Ravi
  • date:December, 2023
  • Project Management, UX strategy, UI design, User Research, Prototype

This is a project made for my sister Poorna Ravi, a 21-year-old student pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Textile Technology.

"How might we help Poorna finish her project for academic requirements?"


Objective

Poorna seeks to fulfill her academic requirements by submitting a project on UI/UX design. She desires guidance and support from experienced UX designers to navigate the project effectively.

User Persona

  • Name:Poorna
  • Age:21
  • Education: Pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Textile Technology (B.Tech)

Needs

Academic Requirement:

Poorna aims to meet the criteria set by her educational institution while exploring the principles of user-centric design.

Guidance and Support:

She desires mentorship and assistance in understanding UX design within the context of her academic pursuits.

Exploration of Design Principles:

Poorna is interested in exploring design principles within the realm of textile technology.

Clarification of Career Intentions:

While engaging in UI/UX design, Poorna does not intend to pursue it as a primary career path.

Problem Statement

A UI/UX certification student needs a concise project to consolidate her learning. The challenge is to create an app that seamlessly merges theoretical concepts with practical application.

Solution

Create a streamlined mobile application integrating core UI/UX principles. Offer interactive modules covering essential topics such as user research and prototyping. Implement real-world examples for practical learning. Ensure user-friendly design and incorporate feedback mechanisms for continuous improvement.

Implementation

Research:

Conducted research on UI/UX principles and best practices.

Design:

Developed wireframes and prototypes for the WeChat app, focusing on user-friendly navigation and intuitive interface.

Development:

Collaborated with developers to implement the design and integrate interactive modules.

Testing:

Conducted usability testing to gather feedback and iterate on the design based on user insights.

Refinement:

Incorporated feedback from users and stakeholders to refine the app's features and functionality.

Results

Successfully created a mobile application for WeChat that serves as a valuable learning tool for UI/UX certification students like Poorna.

Received positive feedback from users on the user-friendly design and interactive modules.

Poorna successfully submitted the project to her educational institution, meeting the academic requirements.

Lessons Learned

Importance of user research and testing in the design process.
The value of incorporating real-world examples for practical learning.
Continuous improvement through feedback and iteration.

Conclusion

The UI/UX design project for WeChat provided an opportunity to apply theoretical concepts in a practical setting, fulfilling the academic requirements of Poorna and providing valuable learning experiences for UI/UX certification students.
